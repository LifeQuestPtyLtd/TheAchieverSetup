"The Achiever" by LifeQuest

Setup Guide — you're 5 minutes from a fully automated year.
Welcome. "The Achiever" is a dark-themed goal and habit tracking system built in Google Sheets by LifeQuest, complete with automated daily emails, a live XP and tied ranking system, and a 12-month habit tracker. Follow the simple steps below to make it operational in your Google account.
YOUR PRODUCT IS READY
Click below to open The Achiever in Google Sheets
You'll be taken to the spreadsheet in read-only view. From there, follow Step 1 below to save your own copy to Google Drive.
🔗  Access The Achiever
1

Make Your Copy

Click the link you received after purchase. You'll see the spreadsheet open in read-only view.

  1. Go to File → Make a copy
  2. Name it whatever you like (e.g. "The Achiever" Goal Tracker 2026)
  3. Choose where to save it in your Google Drive
  4. Click OK

You now have your own personal copy. It lives in your Google Drive and is entirely yours.

2

Configure Your Email

LifeQuest sends you a motivational email every morning and a personalised summary every evening. First-time setup takes about 30 seconds.

  1. In your copy, go to Extensions → Apps Script
  2. In the function dropdown at the top, select setupEmailConfig
  3. Click the Run button ▶
  4. Google will ask you to authorise the script — click Review Permissions, choose your Google account, and click Allow
  5. A popup will confirm your email config has been created

Your email address is filled in automatically from your Google account. To change your name (used in the emails), go to View → Hidden Sheets → Show _CONFIG_, edit the Your Name cell, then hide the sheet again.

3

Automate Your Copy

Triggers are what make The Achiever automatic — they tell Google when to run each function. This is a one-time setup that takes about 2 minutes.

Still in Extensions → Apps Script:

  1. Click the clock icon in the left sidebar (Triggers)
  2. Click + Add Trigger (bottom right)
  3. Add each of the following 5 triggers:
# Function Source Type Time
1 onEdit From spreadsheet On edit —
2 midnightRefresh Time-driven Day timer Midnight to 1am
3 midnightMonthRefresh Time-driven Month timer, Day 1 Midnight to 1am
4 sendMorningEmail Time-driven Day timer 7am to 8am
5 sendEveningEmail Time-driven Day timer 8pm to 9pm

Each trigger may ask you to authorise separately — click Allow each time. Google needs permission to send emails and run scheduled tasks on your behalf.

4

Fill In Your Goals

Open the GOALS tab. You have 10 goal slots. For each goal, fill in:

  • Goal — what you want to achieve this year
  • Category — which area of life it belongs to
  • Target / Current — your starting point and what you're aiming for
  • Deadline — when you want to achieve it by
  • Priority — 🔴 Critical earns 250 XP, 🟠 High 200 XP, 🟡 Medium 150 XP, 🟢 Low 100 XP
  • Status — update this as you progress throughout the year
5

Set Up Your Habits

Open any monthly tab (e.g. JAN) and fill in your 10 daily habits:

  • Habit — what you're tracking each day
  • Linked Goal — which of your goals this habit supports (optional)
  • Difficulty — determines your daily XP reward:

🟢 Easy → 1 XP  ·  🟡 Medium → 2 XP  ·  🔴 Hard → 3 XP  ·  🟣 Epic → 5 XP

Tick the checkbox each day when you complete a habit. Your streak, XP, and rank all update automatically.

✦

The Ranking System

As you complete habits and achieve goals, you earn XP. Your rank on the Dashboard updates automatically as your XP grows. There are 6 ranks to progress through:

  🍼
Novice — where every journey begins. 0 XP to start.
 ♟️
Apprentice — you're showing up consistently. Requires 250 XP.
🔱
Prodigy — real momentum is building. Requires 750 XP.
🏆
Champion — you're operating at a high level. Requires 1,800 XP.
⚔️
Legend — sustained excellence over months. Requires 3,500 XP.
👑
Master — the pinnacle. Reserved for those who commit fully to the year. Requires 7,000 XP.

To reach Master, you'd need to consistently complete medium-to-epic difficulty habits daily across at least 6 months, and achieve several of your goals. It's designed to be genuinely earned — not handed out.
